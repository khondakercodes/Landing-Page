Product Management: Turning Ideas Into Reality

Journey Thus Far.. I started my product journey at the Federal Reserve, and it couldn't have been more rewarding. Right away, I was immersed in fintech products that navigated complex regulatory environments and delivered time-saving functionality to our regulatory analysts. Here, I learned what makes a product manager good, great, and then perfect. It gave me a love for product management, seeing the product as a living, breathing entity that needs care and attention. During my time there, I grew a product that reduced manual work by 70% by automating redundant tasks.

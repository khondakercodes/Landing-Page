Product Management

Turning Ideas Into Reality.. Product management is like taking an idea from start to finish, while having almost no starting idea of what happens in between. Figuring out the middle is the fun of it!

I started my product journey at the Federal Reserve, and it couldn't have been more rewarding. Right away, I was immersed in regulatory fintech products that worked to deliver time-saving functionality to our regulatory analysts.

Some of the highlights during my time:

  • I further enhanced the product to reduce 25% of the remaining manual work for the analysts, through new automated features
  • Product stability and control was uplifted, by analyzing our bug tickets I saw where we could improve, thus bringing down incoming production bugs by around 80%
  • As part process engineer, I reduced bespoke features in the product leading to a much smoother process when adding new regulatory reports.
